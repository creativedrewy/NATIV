The Pain

The Problem

There's no good or easy way to display NFTs
on your wallpaper.

  • Manually saving images and setting them is tedious
  • You're stuck with a single, static image — boring!
  • Animations like videos and GIFs? Completely unsupported.
The Solution

NATIV Solves This

  • Connect your wallet
  • Choose your favorite NFTs from your collection
  • Pick from a library of stunning included wallpapers
  • Instantly flex on your wallpaper or lock screen — with style!
  • Images, videos, GIFs — they just work
